I also mentioned that I'm the biggest Miami Heat fan in the world. I fell in love with the game of basketball in the year 2013, when my uncle was watching the NBA finals matchup between the Spurs and the Heat at a wedding. I was so bored out of my mind at said wedding, I just wanted something else to take my attention. It was the final quarter of regulation in Game 6. I was so immersed in the game, I couldn't believe what I was seeing. Miami down three, LeBron takes a shot. Miss, rebound Bosh and back out to Ray Allen, his three pointer, BANG! My 12 year old brain could not process anything that was going on, but I was excited for some reason. That's when I fell in love with the game, with the team. Also, F*** the Lakers.
